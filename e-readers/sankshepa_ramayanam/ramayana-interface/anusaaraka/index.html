Sankshipta Ramayana



'Sankshipta Ramayana' is a Reader for Sanskrit published by Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan. This is an excellent book for people who are initiated into reading Sanskrit texts.
We produce an user friendly interface to this book, which helps readers to control the amount of information(hide the unnecessary information) being displayed and concentrate only on the information that he needs to decipher the text.
